Simon Game:

  • Click on START to start the game
  • The first signal will flash and make a sound. Your turn: click on the colour that just flashed. Memorize this colour. The level number will increase.
  • Remember the whole sequence as the number of signals increase.
  • The game will randomly flash and sound a second signal. Your turn: click on the first colour plus the second colour that was just signaled. The level will increase again.
  • Each time the game flashes a colour, you have to add this new colour to the previous sequence.
  • If you get a colour wrong in the sequence: GAME OVER. Click to re-start.
